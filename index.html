Hoarding Cleanup Process

Our approach to hoarding cleaning focuses on establishing mutual trust and respect with the individual and their representatives before any cleaning begins. We have the equipment and knowhow to clean up any case of hoarding no matter how small or extreme and we make sure that all affected persons are an integral part of the cleaning process.

Items that are to be kept are separated and organized away from items that are to be disposed. We will donate, recycle or dispose of all unwanted items in an environmentally friendly manner.

Place can be disinfected and sanitized to eliminate any bacteria, viruses, fungi, mould, mildew, odours, and other harmful microorganisms.
